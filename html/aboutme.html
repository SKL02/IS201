About Me

For a dreamer, night's the only time of day

I started at Brigham Young University in 2020 and am a Sophmore, working towards a Bachelors of Science in Marketing. I am passionate about public relations, which I hope to pursue as a career. My dream job is doing in-house publicity for either Marvel, NASA, or Ikea, or working as a celebrity publicist.

I am particularly passionate about the role and perception of fangirls in both the industry they follow, and society as a whole. For more on this, see the video below, or listen to this podcast

Recently, I have been fascinated with the implications of style as a concept. I attended a Harry Styles Love on Tour concert earlier this year. The concert opens with the beginning of Golden, the first track on his sophmore album, "Fine Line", with a poem overlaid. I believe everyone has individual style, not just where fashion is concerned, and leaning into their personal style will only be beneficial to the individual. An excerpt from the poem can be found below.
